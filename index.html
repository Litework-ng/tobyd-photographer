About the Artist

Omoruyi
Eghosa

Born in Lagos, Nigeria in 1997, Omoruyi Eghosa—known as TobyDphotographer—is a self-taught multidisciplinary artist whose practice exists at the luminous threshold between photography and Old Master painting. What began as a personal exploration with a camera has evolved into one of the most distinctive visual languages in contemporary African art.

Trained as a mechanical engineer, Toby brings a precision of thought to the controlled chaos of light, shadow, and texture. His early encounters with the Renaissance masters—Caravaggio's dramatic contrasts, Rembrandt's psychological depth, Vermeer's handling of light—catalysed a vision: to create images that feel painted, but breathe with the immediacy of the photographic moment.

"I am not photographing subjects. I am painting souls. Every fold of fabric, every catch of light on skin, is a brushstroke in a language older than the camera."

His practice is deeply personal and profoundly political—every self-portrait is a declaration: that the Black body is regal, that Black emotion deserves the visual vocabulary that European masters reserved for emperors and saints. Based in Lagos, Toby founded The Den of Artists, a collective that nurtures emerging visual talent across Nigeria.

What I Offer

Services & Commissions

I.

Artistic Portrait Session

A bespoke portrait experience created in the Painterly tradition. You come as yourself; you leave as a Renaissance subject. Sessions incorporate dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, curated drapery, and post-production treatment that transforms your photograph into something approaching a painted masterwork. Prints available in museum archival quality.

Commission-based · By Inquiry
II.

Editorial & Brand

Atmospheric editorial campaigns, brand identity shoots, and commercial work executed with the same philosophical depth as personal practice. Clients seeking work that transcends the ordinary — fashion, luxury goods, cultural institutions — are invited to discuss a collaboration. Based in Lagos, available internationally.

Day Rate Available · By Inquiry
III.

Limited Edition Prints

Each image in the collection is available as a numbered, signed archival pigment print, produced at fine art standards. Editions are intentionally small — never more than ten — to honour the integrity of each work. Certificates of authenticity accompany every print. NFT editions available on Foundation.app, SuperRare, and TransientLabs.

From $800 USD · Limited Editions
