The Artist

Omoruyi Eghosa

Born in Lagos, Nigeria in 1997, Omoruyi Eghosa — known as TobyDphotographer — is a self-taught multidisciplinary artist whose practice exists at the luminous threshold between photography and Old Master painting.

Trained as a mechanical engineer, Toby brings the precision of analytical thought to the controlled chaos of light, shadow, and texture. His early encounters with the Renaissance masters — Caravaggio's dramatic contrasts, Rembrandt's psychological depth — catalysed a vision: to create images that feel painted, but breathe with the immediacy of the photographic moment.

"I am not photographing subjects. I am painting souls. Every fold of fabric, every catch of light on skin, is a brushstroke in a language older than the camera."

His practice is deeply personal and profoundly political — every self-portrait insists that the Black body is regal, that Black emotion deserves the visual vocabulary European masters reserved for emperors and saints. Based in Lagos, Toby founded The Den of Artists, a collective nurturing emerging visual talent across Nigeria.

Self-Portrait Chiaroscuro Renaissance Lagos, Nigeria The Den of Artists Photography × Painting
"To render Black skin in the language of Caravaggio is not appropriation — it is reclamation. It is the insistence that we were always worthy of that light." — Omoruyi Eghosa · Artist Statement, 2022
What I Offer

Services & Commissions

I.
Artistic Portrait Session

A bespoke portrait experience in the Painterly tradition. Sessions incorporate dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, curated drapery, and post-production treatment that transforms your photograph into something approaching a painted masterwork. Museum-quality archival prints available.

Commission-based · By Inquiry
II.
Editorial & Brand

Atmospheric editorial campaigns and commercial work executed with the same philosophical depth as personal practice. For clients seeking imagery that transcends the ordinary — fashion, luxury goods, cultural institutions. Based in Lagos, available internationally.

Day Rate Available · By Inquiry
III.
Limited Edition Prints

Each image available as a numbered, signed archival pigment print. Editions are intentionally small — never more than ten — to honour the integrity of each work. NFT editions on Foundation.app, SuperRare, and TransientLabs.
